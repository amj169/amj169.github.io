Dissertation: “We’re Still Processing: Understanding ADHD Writers and their Writing Processes”

I am a fifth-year doctoral candidate in the Department of English at the University of Pittsburgh, where my research sits at the intersection of Disability Studies and Composition Pedagogy. My work examines the writing processes of neurodivergent student writers and grapples with process efficiency as it is related to writing and generative AI.
